Chapter One

The Journey

Every step led somewhere meaningful

Early Years

🌸 Childhood

The days of innocence, curiosity and wonder. Running through sunlit fields, making paper planes, and believing the world was infinite. Every little thing felt like magic — the smell of rain, the warmth of home, and the sound of laughter echoing through the streets.

Where it all began.
School Days

📚 School Days

The first friendships that taught me what loyalty means. The classrooms where dreams started forming. The first heartbreaks, the first victories, and the first lessons that textbooks never taught. Chalk dust in the air, games in the rain, and promises we thought would last forever.

Where bonds were forged.
Engineering

⚡ Engineering

Late nights, early mornings, and the beautiful chaos of figuring out who I really am. The hostel rooms that became home, the friends who became family, and the assignments that pushed me to my limits. This was where passion met persistence, and dreams started taking shape.

Where dreams found direction.
First Job

💼 First Job

Stepping into the real world — a mix of excitement and anxiety. Learning that success isn't just about grades, it's about showing up every day. The first paycheck, the first mistakes that taught more than any degree, and the realization that adulthood is both terrifying and beautiful.

Where reality met ambition.
Dreaming

✨ Dreams

The quiet moments when I dared to imagine something bigger. Aviation, cryogenics, technology — fields that most people said were impossible for someone like me. But dreams don't care about logic. They care about the heart that holds them.

Where imagination took flight.
Now

🌅 Current Journey

Right here, right now. Still learning, still growing, still making mistakes and still standing up. The journey isn't over — it's just getting started. Every day brings a new chance to write another chapter worth remembering.

Where the story continues.
Tomorrow

🚀 Future Goals

The horizon stretches infinitely. Indian Oil, building something that matters, traveling to places that exist only in my imagination right now. The future isn't written yet, and that's the most beautiful part — I get to choose what comes next.

Where the story is yet to be written.

than the destination."
